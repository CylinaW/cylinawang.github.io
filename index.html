◎ what drives the work
A biotic future

Biology as a general-purpose technology — reading, revising, engineering what it means to be alive. Engineered microbes, cellular circuits, tissues that integrate into devices.

The translation problem

Alzheimer's biomarkers appear decades before symptoms. We still lack interventions that act before irreversible damage. Closing that gap is what motivates my work.

Consciousness, open

Science maps neural correlates. Philosophy asks what shape it takes. Theatre makes me live it from the inside. Three angles. Same problem.
